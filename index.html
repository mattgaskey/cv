Works

I've been working a lot lately.

Scroll down to see a complete life, school and work history:

  • 1983 Born in Salisbury, NC, the 4th of July. Fireworks ensue.
  • 2001 Graduated Trinity High School. Left the nest for the big city. Began Univeristy of North Carolina at Charlotte.

    Developed a love for cooking, foreign literature and languages, classical and jazz music. Worked numerous jobs which no longer need to be mentioned, but with which most college kids are familiar.
  • 2005 Graduated Magna Cum Laude with a BA in Philosophy from UNCC. Philosophers in short demand.

    Toured the state as a musician, playing solo gigs and sitting in with a hodgepodge of bands and other artist types.
  • 2007 Learn the cliche of starving artist to be more fact than fiction. Begin work in musical instrument retail with Music and Arts in Charlotte, NC.

    Book-keeper, guitar-tuner, spring-bender and all-around handy-man for classical and modern instrument sales. See that retail is more than just about sales. The customer experience is key. People will come back if they feel a personal touch on the service you give.
  • 2009 Return to University to fulfill lifelong dream of living abroad. Post-Bachelor work in French, Italian and Portuguese with the hopes of continuing through an exchange or work program.
  • 2010 Accepted as a teaching assistant in Lille, France. Before leaving, get the opportunity to do French-English translation work for the Bechtler Museum of Modern Art in Charlotte, NC.
  • 2011 Complete personal project of translating an entire novel from French to English: Boris Vian's "Elles se rendent pas compte". Small publishing house in San Francisco interested, but unable to secure the rights from Vian's estate.
  • 2012 Teaching assistant contracts finished, I decide to pursue a Masters in Linguistics. Begin studies at Université de Lille 3. Also begin freelance teaching position with ETC Média Formation in Villeneuve d'Ascq, France.

    One-on-one classes with business students, alongside private lessons with middle- and high-school-aged students, alongside Masters thesis work proves a challenge.
  • 2014 Challenge accepted. Master's thesis completed, degree earned. Compiled a database of recordings from students in their native language and in English. Compared metrics of fluency to determine quantitative and qualitative similarities in speech patterns across native and second language for a better understanding of fluency rating.

    Linguists surprisingly in short demand. Move to Romania. (Learn the language just for fun)
  • 2015 Data analysis skills put to use in marketing as Web Content Specialist for Keepcalling.com in Sibiu, Romania. Manage 70+ ecommerce stores' analytics, tag management and user engagment tracking systems. Handle content updates to the same.

    In doing so, discover a fascination with how web sites function. (Also, unrelatedly, discover the joys of homebrewing)
  • 2016 Begin FreeCodeCamp's course of self-study in Front End Development. Learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript/JQuery, Bootstrap. Work is project-based, autonomous and encourages research and trial-by-fire; one of my favorite ways of learning.

    The wife and I purchase a house. (Also, completely relatedly, discover the joys of woodworking)
